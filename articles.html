Fama, Eugene F . My Life in Finance[J]. Annual Review of Financial Economics, 2011, 3(1):1-15.

Introduction: Eugene F. Fama is Finance professor at the University of Chicago, and Nobel Laureate in Economics, 2013. He is also known as Father of Mondern Finance for this contributions in Efficient Markets,Event Studies, Three-Factor Model. His work in Three-factor model has also been widely cited in emperical research in marketing, known as Marketing-Finance Interface, one the the several mainstreams in preminer marketing journals. This paper is an autobiography to the Annual Review of Financial Economics by Fama.
